Women's Cuts

II

Our Story

Born into
the craft.

Scarlet Hair Studio was founded in 1978 by David and Wendy Brush. Their son Tavis was quite literally born into the business — growing up surrounded by scissors, colour and the hum of a busy salon.

In 1999 Tavis began his apprenticeship, and met Sarah when she visited for work experience. Both went on to train at award-winning salons before returning to purchase the studio in 2006 and make it their own.

Today, Tavis and Sarah lead a team that shares their philosophy: willful listening, genuine care, and a fun relaxed environment that still delivers genuinely skilled, professional results.
